Exempt-purpose limitations 
Notwithstanding any other provision of these Articles, the Liberation Fleet Co. shall not carry on any activities not permitted to be carried on (a) by a corporation exempt from federal income tax under Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code, or (b) by a corporation contributions to which are deductible under Section 170(c)(2) of the Internal Revenue Code.
No part of the net earnings of the Liberation Fleet Co. shall inure to the benefit of, or be distributable to, its directors, officers, or other private persons, except that the Liberation Fleet Co. may pay reasonable compensation for services rendered and make payments and distributions in furtherance of its purposes.
No substantial part of the activities of the Liberation Fleet Co. shall be the carrying on of propaganda, or otherwise attempting to influence legislation, and the Liberation Fleet Co. shall not participate in, or intervene in (including the publishing or distribution of statements), any political campaign on behalf of (or in opposition to) any candidate for public office.
