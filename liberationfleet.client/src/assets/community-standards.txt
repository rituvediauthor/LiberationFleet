LIBERATION FLEET COMMUNITY STANDARDS
(Acceptable Use Policy)

Last updated: July 15, 2026

Liberation Fleet is for adults (18+) coordinating mutual aid. By using the Service you agree to these standards together with the Terms of Use and Privacy Policy.

1. AGE
You must be at least 18 years old. Accounts for or depicting minors in a sexual context are forbidden.

2. ILLEGAL CONTENT — ZERO TOLERANCE
You may not create, share, request, or store:
- Child sexual abuse material (CSAM), child sexual exploitation, grooming, or online enticement of a minor
- Child sex trafficking content
- Non-consensual intimate images (including deepfakes of a real person)
- Credible threats of violence or content that facilitates violent crime
- Other material that is illegal under United States federal law

Reports of child sexual exploitation are treated as highest priority. When you report such content, a temporary plaintext evidence snapshot may be retained so we can file required reports to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) CyberTipline.

3. HARASSMENT AND ABUSE
Do not stalk, harass, dox, or target other users. Use Block and Report. Crew kick proposals are for crew governance; they do not replace platform reports for illegal content.

4. SPAM AND SCAMS
Do not use the Service for unsolicited spam, phishing, or financial fraud. Do not misrepresent gift amounts, donation activity, or identities to deceive crewmates.

5. ENCRYPTION AND REPORTS
Most crew, fleet, chat, forum, proposal, DM, and media content is end-to-end encrypted. We cannot read it in normal operation. If you Report content, your device may send a one-time decrypted snapshot of that item (and limited metadata) sealed for safety review. Routine chat is not monitored.

6. ATTACHMENTS AND ADULT CONTENT
Attach only allowed media types. Do not attempt to upload executable, script, or HTML/SVG payloads. Honor adult-content labels and gates; 18+ material must still comply with these standards and the law.

7. MUTUAL AID AND MONEY
Peer gifts happen on third-party payment apps. Liberation Fleet records coordination data; it does not guarantee payment. Optional app donations are processed by Stripe and support the Service—not peer recipients—unless a campaign expressly states otherwise.

8. ENFORCEMENT
We may freeze accounts, remove or quarantine content identifiers, preserve report evidence, and report apparent child sexual exploitation to NCMEC when we have actual knowledge. We may refuse service to anyone who violates these standards or the Terms of Use.

9. CONTACT
Use in-app Report for abusive or illegal content. For account issues, contact the operators through the channel published on our website when available.

These standards are part of your agreement to use Liberation Fleet together with the Terms of Use and Privacy Policy.
